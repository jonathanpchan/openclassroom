User Manual

[Overview]

For a student, finding the ideal place to study can be a reoccurring issue. With the inception of OpenClassroom, our goal is to minimize students' already high stress levels by a simple solution to finding optimal classrooms to study in. Our application will be able to search for classrooms based on a specific time, a specific building, or by a "now" function that will list all available classrooms that are open at the time of query.

[Registration]

To register, you will need a valid e-mail address.
  1. Click on the register tab on the top right corner of the navigation bar.
  2. Enter your name.
  3. Enter a username. (Other users will be able to identify you by this.)
  4. Enter a valid e-mail address.
  5. Create a password.
  6. Click submit!
  7. Congratulations! You will now be able to login and access the services provided.

[Login]

To login, you will need to have a registered account.
  1. Click on the login tab on the top right corner of the navigation bar.
  2. Enter your e-mail address.
  3. Enter your password.
  4. Click submit!
