User Manual

Overview

For a student, finding the ideal place to study can be a reoccurring issue. With the inception of OpenClassroom, our goal is to minimize students' already high stress levels by a simple solution to finding optimal classrooms to study in. Our application will be able to search for classrooms based on a specific time, a specific building, or by a "now" function that will list all available classrooms that are open at the time of query.

Registration

To register, you will need a valid e-mail address.

  1. Click on the 'Register' tab.
  2. Enter your name.
  3. Enter a username. (Other users will be able to identify you by this.)
  4. Enter a valid e-mail address.
  5. Create a password.
  6. Click submit.
  7. Congratulations! You will now be able to login and access our provided services.

Login

To login, you will need to have a registered account.

  1. Click on the 'Login' tab.
  2. Enter your e-mail address.
  3. Enter your password.
  4. Click submit.

Adding Your Schedule

  1. Click on the 'Schedule' tab.
  2. Click "Add Course".
  3. Enter course information.
  4. Click Accept.

Searching For a Classroom

  1. Click on the 'Find Classroom' tab.
  2. Select a building from the provided list.
  3. Select 'Right Now'
  4. Select 'By Building'
    1. Select a day.
    2. Browse the times by horizontally scrolling.
  5. Select 'By Time'
    1. Select a day.
    2. Adjust the time slider.
    3. Browse the times by horizontally scrolling.

Finding a Study Buddy

  1. Click on the 'Schedule' tab.
  2. Click 'Finalize Schedule'
  3. Allow 24 hours for potential study buddies to appear.
  4. Click on the 'Study Buddy' tab.
  5. Select one of your courses.
  6. Select a study buddy.
  7. Start a conversation!

Viewing a Room's Information

  1. Search for a classroom via 'By Building' or 'By Time'.
    1. Click the room #.
  2. Search for a classroom via 'By Now'.
    1. Click anywhere on the rooms that were found.
