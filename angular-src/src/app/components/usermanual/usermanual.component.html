User Manual

Overview

For a student, finding the ideal place to study can be a reoccurring and tedious problem. With the inception of OpenClassroom, our goal is to minimize students' stress levels by providing a simple solution to finding optimal classrooms to study in. Our application allows you to search for classrooms based on a specific time, building, or by the current time. Not only that, but each room's info page allows user-specific feedback for each classroom on campus so that you can access and contribute valuable information. Users are also able to upload their own schedule and opt-in to our Study Buddy feature that allows for students to find study buddies in the same course.

Registration

To register, you will need a valid e-mail address.

  1. Click on the 'Register' tab.


  2. Fill out the form with valid information.


  3. Click submit.
  4. Congratulations! You will now be able to login and access our provided services.


Login

To login, you will need to have a registered account.

  1. Click on the 'Login' tab.


  2. Enter your e-mail address and password.


  3. After clicking login, you will be redirected to your schedule page!


Adding Your Schedule

  1. Click on the 'Schedule' tab.


  2. Click 'Add Course'.


  3. Enter course information.


  4. Click 'Yes'.


  5. Review your courses.


Searching For a Classroom

  1. Click on the 'Find Classroom' tab.


  2. Select a building from the list provided.


  3. Select 'Right Now'.


  4. Select 'By Building'.
    1. Select a day.
    2. Browse the times by horizontally scrolling.


  5. Select 'By Time'.
    1. Select a day.
    2. Adjust the time slider.


    3. Browse the times by horizontally scrolling.


Finding a Study Buddy

  1. On your schedule page, click 'Finalize Schedule' in the top right corner.


  2. Click 'Yes'.


  3. Allow 24 hours for potential study buddies to appear.
  4. Click on the 'Study Buddy' tab.


  5. Select one of your courses.
  6. Choose a study buddy and click!


  7. Start a conversation!


Viewing a Room's Information

  1. Search for a classroom via 'By Building' or 'By Time'.


    1. Click the room # and the room's information will be displayed below.


  2. Search for a classroom via 'By Now'.


    1. Click anywhere on the rooms that were found and the room's information will be displayed below.
